All Natural Lasagna Recipe

This recipe uses all natural ingredients blended together to make an insatiable meal. Low calories and healthy for the family!!!

ingredients needed

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pasta Shells

Cooking Instructions

  1. Lay your shells in a pan
  2. Put a layer or sauce on the shells
  3. Add another layer of shells
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 until there are a total of 4 layers of shells
  5. Bake in the oven with 350 degree Farenheit for 2 hours
  6. Top it off with some parmesagne cheese
  7. Enjoy :)!!!!!!
