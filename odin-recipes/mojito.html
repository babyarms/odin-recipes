Mojito

Mid-Summer Mojito

Enjoy this perfectly blended cocktail with friends and family. Can be made frozen or on the rocks, depends on your mood.

ingredients needed

  • Tequila
  • Mint Leaves
  • Lime Juice
  • Club Soda

Mixing Instructions

  1. Pour 2 shot of Tequila into the glass
  2. Cut up 3 mint leaves and submerge them into the tequila
  3. Pour 1 shot of limejuice in to the mix
  4. Fill the rest with club soda until the glass is full
